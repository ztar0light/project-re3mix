Requirements

Base Printer

The printer you are converting must have exposed extrusions and not covered-up ones. This would apply to printers like the SE lineup of Enders.

Additional

Klipper is recommended. However, Marlin is fine.

Needed Parts

Core XY

  • 8x idler GT2 pulley with bearing
  • 2x teethed idler GT2 pulley with bearing
  • GT2 pulley

Structure

  • 1KG of your filament of choice (PETG-CF or ABS is recommended)
  • 2x V-slot wheels
